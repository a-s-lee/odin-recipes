Protein Oatmeal

Image from eatingbirdfood!

Ingredients
  • 1 cup - oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup - water
  • 1 sliced banana
  • 1 tbsp of peanut better

Instructions
  1. Put cup of oatmeal into a bowl
  2. Add the 1/2 cup of water (milks works as well)
  3. Cover the bowl and microwave for 2 minutes
  4. Stir and wait 2 minutes for it to rest (Make sure oatmeal doesn't explode!)
  5. Add fruit and peanut butter (1 scoop of whey protein works as well)
